Fluffly Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Cook a yummy plate of fluffy scrambled eggs for breakfast or for any meal of the day.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil or butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 green onion
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and mix or whisk until well blended with no separation of egg whites and yolk. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. Chop green onions and set aside. Heat pan on medium low heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Once pan is hot enough, place green onions and cook for about a minute and then add eggs into pan.
  4. Slowly and gently move eggs around in pan until not runny anymore. Cook until desired doneness and remove from pan. Your scrambled eggs are ready to eat!

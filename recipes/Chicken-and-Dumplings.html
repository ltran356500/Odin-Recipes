Chicken and Dumplings Recipe

Hearty Bowl of Soup For A Fall and Winter Day

A recipe for a heart warming and cozy feeling bowl of chicken and dumpling soup that can be enjoy when the weather calls for a hug and full stomach.

A soup filled with chunks of chicken, light flavorful dumplings, soft veggies and soul-warming broth.

Soup Ingredients

  • 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup diced carrot
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 lb bone-in chicken breasts
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • Whatever spices/seasonings
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas

Dumplings Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp cold butter
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1 cup buttermilk

Directions

  1. Pour olive oil into instant pot and select Saute function. Cook onion, carrot, celery and bay leaf until veggies are soft and onion translucent. Roughly 5 minutes.
  2. Add broth, chicken thighs and breast, spices/seasonings. Close and lock the lid. Select high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 9 minutes and allow 10-15 minutes for pressure to build.
  3. Release pressure carefully using quick release method based on manufacturer's instructions for about 5 minutes. Carefully remove chicken pieces with tongs and place in bowl to cool slightly. Discard bay leaf.
  4. Mash butter and flour together until a smooth paste and set aside.
  5. Combine flour, baking powder and salt in bowl for dumplings. Cut in cold butter until mixture is texture of cornmeal. Stir in parsley and set aside.
  6. Shred cooled chicken and return to pot. Taste and season broth if needed. Add peas and stir in flour-butter paste. Select Saute function to bring broth back to boil.
  7. Pour buttermilk into dumpling mixture and stir until mixed. Drop dumpling dough by heaping spoonfuls on top of stew; a small cookie scoop works well.
  8. Cover pot with lid, leaving steam vent open. Select Slow Cooker function and simmer on Low, covered, until dumplings are cooked through (10-12 minutes). Insert skewer into center of a dumpling and it should come out clean.

Chicken and Dumplings Recipe

Hearty Bowl of Soup For A Fall and Winter Day

A recipe for a heart warming and cozy feeling bowl of chicken and dumpling soup that can be enjoy when the weather calls for a hug and full stomach. A soup filled with chunks of chicken, light flavorful dumplings, soft veggies and soul-warming broth.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup diced carrot
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 lb boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 lb bone-in chicken breasts
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
